## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Register as an owner">
    Go to the Ubik registration page and select **Establecimiento** (Establishment/Owner) when prompted to choose your account type.

    Fill in the owner registration form with:
    - **Username** — minimum 3 characters, must be unique
    - **Email** — a valid email address you can verify
    - **Phone number** — 7–15 digits, international format supported (e.g. `+573001234567`)
    - **Date of birth** — you must be at least 18 years old
    - **Password** — minimum 8 characters
    - **Confirm password** — must match your chosen password

    Optionally, click **Obtener ubicación actual** to capture your current GPS coordinates. This helps Ubik surface your property to nearby guests.

    <Note>
      Owner accounts are assigned `roleId: 2` internally, which unlocks the property management dashboard and the ability to create motels.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify your account">
    After submitting the registration form, you will be redirected to the login page. Check your email inbox for a verification message from Ubik and follow the link to confirm your address.

    Once verified, log in with your username and password. Tick **Remember me** if you want to stay logged in on this device.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your motel">
    From your dashboard, navigate to **Create Motel**. You will need to complete four sections:

    **Basic details**
    - Motel name (required, minimum 3 characters)
    - Full street address (required)
    - City (required)
    - Phone number (optional)
    - Description (optional but strongly recommended)

    **Location**
    Click **Obtener ubicación actual** to auto-fill latitude and longitude, or enter coordinates manually. Accurate coordinates are used to display your motel on the map.

    **Images**
    - Upload a **cover/profile photo** (required) — this is the main image guests see in search results
    - Upload additional **gallery images** (optional) — select multiple files at once

    **Legal documents & owner info**
    - **RNT** — Registro Nacional de Turismo certificate (required, image file)
    - **RUES** — Registro Único Empresarial y Social (required, image file)
    - **Legal document** — additional ownership document (optional, image or PDF)
    - Owner document type: `CC`, `NIT`, `CE`, or `PASAPORTE`
    - Owner document number (required)
    - Owner full name (required)
    - Legal representative name (optional)

    Click **Crear motel** when all required fields are complete.

    <Warning>
      Both RNT and RUES files are **required** before you can submit. The form will block submission and show an error if either is missing.
    </Warning>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Wait for approval">
    After submission, your motel enters the review queue. The approval lifecycle has four statuses:

    | Status | Meaning |
    |---|---|
    | `PENDING` | Submitted, awaiting initial review |
    | `UNDER_REVIEW` | An admin is actively reviewing your application |
    | `APPROVED` | Your motel is live and visible to guests |
    | `REJECTED` | The application was declined — see `rejectionReason` for details |

    You will be notified by email when your status changes. If rejected, review the reason provided and resubmit with the corrected information.

    <Tip>
      Speed up approval by ensuring your RNT and RUES documents are clear, legible scans. Blurry or incomplete documents are the most common rejection reason.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add rooms">
    Once your motel status is `APPROVED`, you can add rooms from your property dashboard. Navigate to your motel and click **Add Room**.

    For each room you need:
    - A room number or code (e.g. `A-101`)
    - Room type: `STANDARD`, `SUITE`, `DELUXE`, or `VIP`
    - Price per night (must be greater than 0)
    - Description
    - At least one photo (jpg, png, or webp — max 5 MB each)
    - At least one amenity/service selected from the list

    See the [Room Management guide](/guides/room-management) for full details.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go live">
    With at least one approved room added, your motel becomes fully discoverable by guests in Ubik's explore and search pages.

    Guests can view your motel on the map, browse your room gallery, check amenities, and make reservations.

    Keep your listing accurate by updating room availability, prices, and photos regularly. See [Motel Management](/guides/motel-management) for editing options.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Tips for getting approved quickly

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="RNT — Registro Nacional de Turismo" icon="certificate">
    Obtain your RNT from **Confecámaras** or the Tourism Ministry portal. Your certificate must be current (not expired) and clearly show your establishment name and registration number.
  </Card>
  <Card title="RUES — Registro Único Empresarial" icon="file-contract">
    Download your RUES certificate directly from the **Confecámaras** website. Ensure the business name and NIT/CC on the certificate match the details you enter in the form exactly.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Document quality" icon="image">
    Scan or photograph all documents in good lighting. Files must be legible — reviewers need to read registration numbers and dates. PDF or a high-resolution image (JPG/PNG) is preferred.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Complete legal info" icon="id-card">
    Fill in the legal representative field if your establishment operates under a company. Mismatches between the registered owner and the legal representative are a common delay cause.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  The `hasCompleteLegalInfo` flag on your motel is set automatically by the platform once RNT, RUES, owner document, and owner name are all present. Admins use this flag during review — make sure all fields are filled before submitting.
</Note>

## Managing your account profile

You can update your personal details at any time from the **Edit Profile** page:

- Username, email, and phone number
- Date of birth
- Location (latitude/longitude) — used for proximity-based features
- Anonymous mode — hides your public profile details from guests

<Warning>
  Deleting your profile is permanent and cannot be undone. All associated motels and rooms will also be removed. You will be logged out immediately after deletion.
</Warning>
