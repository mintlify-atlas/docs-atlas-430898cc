Welcome to Ubik. This guide covers everything a guest needs — from creating your account to checking out and managing your bookings.

## Creating your account

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose the Guest account type">
    Go to the Ubik registration page and select **Cliente** (Guest) when prompted to pick your account type. This gives you a guest role and access to browse and book properties.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill in your details">
    Complete the registration form:

    | Field | Required | Notes |
    |---|---|---|
    | Username | Yes | Minimum 3 characters, must be unique |
    | Email | Yes | Used for booking confirmations and account recovery |
    | Phone number | No | Recommended for reservation contact |
    | Date of birth | No | Stored in your profile |
    | Password | Yes | Minimum 8 characters |
    | Confirm password | Yes | Must match |

    You can also click **Share location** to let Ubik capture your current GPS position. This enables the platform to show nearby motels first in search results.

    <Note>
      Your location coordinates are stored in your profile and can be updated at any time from the Edit Profile page. Ubik never shares your exact coordinates with third parties.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify and log in">
    After registering, check your email for a verification link. Once confirmed, return to the login page, enter your username and password, and click **Sign in**.

    Tick **Remember me** to stay logged in on trusted devices.

    If you forget your password, click **Forgot password** on the login page to start the reset flow.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Browsing and finding a room

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the Explore page">
    After logging in, navigate to the **Explore** section from the main menu. You will see a list and/or map view of all approved motels on the platform.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Use the map">
    Ubik uses geolocation to show motels on an interactive map. If you granted location permission during registration or from your profile, the map centers on your current city.

    - Tap a pin on the map to preview a motel
    - Click **View details** to open the full listing

    <Tip>
      Update your location from **Edit Profile → Get current location** before browsing for the most accurate nearby results.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Browse motel details">
    Each motel listing shows:
    - Cover photo and gallery
    - Name, city, and full address
    - Phone number
    - Description
    - Available rooms with type, price, and amenities

    Room types available across the platform:

    | Type | Description |
    |---|---|
    | `STANDARD` | Comfortable standard room |
    | `SUITE` | Larger room with premium furnishings |
    | `DELUXE` | Deluxe amenities and finishes |
    | `VIP` | Top-tier experience with full service |
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a room">
    From the motel detail page, browse the room list. Each room shows:
    - Room number/code
    - Room type
    - Price per night
    - Description
    - Photo gallery
    - Amenities/services included

    Click a room to view its full details and availability.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Making a reservation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Choose your dates">
    On the room detail page, select your check-in and check-out dates. The platform calculates the total price based on the number of nights and the room's nightly rate.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add special requests">
    Use the **Special requests** field to note any preferences or accessibility needs. The motel owner will see these alongside your booking.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Confirm and pay">
    Review your booking summary — motel name, room, dates, and total price — then proceed to payment.

    Reservation statuses you will see:

    | Status | Meaning |
    |---|---|
    | `pending` | Booking submitted, awaiting confirmation |
    | `confirmed` | Motel owner has accepted your reservation |
    | `cancelled` | The reservation has been cancelled |

    <Note>
      You will receive an email confirmation once your reservation status changes to `confirmed`.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Enjoy your stay">
    Arrive at the motel on your check-in date. Show your booking confirmation (available from the **My Reservations** section of your profile) at the front desk.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Managing your profile

Access your profile at any time from the top navigation menu.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="View profile">
    Your profile page shows your current account details:
    - Username and email
    - Phone number
    - Date of birth
    - Current location (if shared)
    - Anonymous mode status
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Edit profile">
    Click **Edit profile** to update your information:

    1. Change your **username**, **email**, or **phone number**
    2. Update your **date of birth**
    3. Click **Get current location** to refresh your GPS coordinates
    4. Toggle **Anonymous mode** to hide your profile from public view
    5. Click **Save** to apply changes

    <Note>
      Your email must remain unique across all Ubik accounts. You will see an error if the address is already registered to another user.
    </Note>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Delete account">
    From the Edit Profile page, scroll to the bottom and click **Delete profile**.

    <Warning>
      Account deletion is permanent and cannot be reversed. All your reservation history and personal data will be removed. You will be logged out immediately.
    </Warning>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Frequently asked questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="I forgot my password. What should I do?">
    On the login page, click **Forgot password**. Enter the email address associated with your account and follow the reset instructions sent to your inbox.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I browse motels without an account?">
    The public motel list (`/motels`) is accessible without authentication. However, making reservations requires a logged-in guest account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I see motels near me?">
    Make sure your location is set in your profile. Go to **Edit Profile** and click **Get current location** to update your coordinates. Ubik uses these to surface nearby properties on the map.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What does Anonymous mode do?">
    When anonymous mode is enabled, your username and profile details are hidden from motel owners and other users. Your booking information is still visible to the motel owner for the reservation to be processed.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I cancel a reservation?">
    Yes. Go to **My Reservations**, find the booking, and click **Cancel**. Cancellation policies vary by motel — check the listing before booking.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact the motel?">
    Each motel listing displays the property's phone number. You can call or message the motel directly using that number.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My username is already taken. What should I choose?">
    Ubik requires unique usernames across all account types. Try adding numbers, initials, or a city abbreviation to make yours unique (e.g. `maria_bog` instead of `maria`).
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How are room prices shown?">
    Prices are listed as a nightly rate in the local currency. The reservation total is calculated as `price × number of nights` and shown before you confirm.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
